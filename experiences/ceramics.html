Jingdezhen · Hangzhou

Ceramics Studio Day

"Clay spins, hands shape, time disappears."

Duration

Full day (6 hours)

Group size

Private, up to 3 people

Host

A studio potter

About this experience

Spend a full day in a working pottery studio. Your host is a practicing ceramicist whose studio sits on a hillside overlooking tea fields. You'll learn to center clay on the wheel, pull walls, and shape forms — or work with hand-building techniques if you prefer. The day ends with tea made in cups the host fired the week before.

What you'll do

  • Learn wheel throwing from a working ceramicist
  • Try hand-building and glazing techniques
  • Tour the kiln and learn about firing processes
  • Take home your finished pieces (shipped after firing)
